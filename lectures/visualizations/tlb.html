TLB — Translation Look-Aside Buffer

Chapter 9 — a small cache of recent page→frame translations. A hit skips the page table (fast); a miss walks it, then caches the result. For the raw translation, see Paging & Address Translation; for the timing impact, the EAT calculator.
🎯 Learning objectives — what you should be able to do (click to minimize)
  • Explain the TLB as a small cache of recent page→frame translations.
  • Distinguish a TLB hit (skip the page table) from a miss (walk it, then cache the result).
  • Trace LRU eviction when the TLB is full.
  • Compute a running hit ratio and see how locality of reference raises it.
